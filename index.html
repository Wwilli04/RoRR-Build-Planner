Risk of Rain Returns Build Planner

By Multiphonix

This is a build planner app for Risk of Rain Returns. Theory-craft your build here first, and never forget how many stacks of lens maker’s glasses you wanted or what your first red item should be.

Click on an item to add it to the current build. Click on an item in the build boxes at the bottom of the screen to remove it. The filters pane allows you to search by item name or category. You can save the current build to your browser’s local storage and retrieve it again whenever you want, even after reloading the page. (This uses your browser’s local storage, so your saved builds will be wiped if you clear your browser cache). You can also download a JSON file of all your locally-saved builds to transfer them to another device, or share them with a friend. Be advised that loading a file will overwrite your locally saved builds.

All item images are from Risk of Rain Returns and are the property of Hopoo Games. Tooltips were taken from the official wiki. For comments, suggestions, or bug reports, please open an issue on GitHub
